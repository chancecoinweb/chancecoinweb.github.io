About

CHANCE coin aims to be fun, exciting and transparent. Each week a lottery is held for those that purchased CHANCE coin that week through Uniswap. The more you buy, the better your odds of winning.

Need help?

Add Polygon Network to MetaMask wallet

By default MetaMask does not come with Polygon in the list of networks provided. To connect to Uniswap on the Polygon network to purchase CHANCE coin, you will need to add Polygon into MetaMask first.

Follow this polygon tutorial.

Add CHANCE to MetaMask wallet

After purchasing CHANCE from Uniswap you won't be able to see it in your wallet unless you let the wallet know about the CHANCE token.

Follow this MetaMask support tutorial and remember to select Polygon as the network within MetaMask first.
